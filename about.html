About Me

Meet the builder behind the bricks.

I work in secondary education by day and a LEGO-loving creator by night. My passion lies in using LEGO builds not just for fun—but as powerful tools for storytelling, STEM learning, and innovation.

Whether I’m building a robotic rover, recreating iconic vehicles, or designing stop-motion sets, I’m always exploring ways to turn plastic bricks into imaginative experiences.

This site is my digital sandbox—a place to showcase creations, share ideas, and hopefully inspire others to pick up a brick and start building.

When I’m not in LEGO mode, you’ll find me mentoring students, building tech curriculum, or finding new ways to make learning more hands-on and engaging.
